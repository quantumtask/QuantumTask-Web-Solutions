Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 March 2026

1. Who We Are

QuantumTask ("we", "us", "our") builds conversion-focused websites for service businesses in South Africa. This policy explains how we handle your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with us:

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone number, and business name that you provide through our forms or WhatsApp.
  • Project details — information about your business, services, and website requirements.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, browser type, and device information collected through standard analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotes.
  • To design and deliver your website project.
  • To send project updates and invoices.
  • To improve our website and services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties only when necessary to deliver our services (e.g., hosting providers, payment processors). All third parties are required to protect your data.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

6. Your Rights (POPIA)

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information we hold.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to the processing of your information.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator.

7. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure proper functionality. We may also use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.
