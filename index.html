FAQ

What is QuantumTask?

QuantumTask is a managed service for agencies and startups that provides full-stack designers and developers on retainer. We take care of front-end, back-end, DevOps and maintenance tasks under your brand.

Do you support back-end and API work?

Yes. We handle Node, Python and modern server-side stacks. We design, build and maintain APIs, data pipelines and infrastructure as code.

How do we monitor hours and progress?

Every retainer includes a dashboard showing hours used, tasks in progress, budgets and milestones. Automated reports keep everyone informed.

What industries do you serve?

SaaS, e-commerce, fintech, logistics, education, nonprofits and more.

Is my data secure?

Least-privilege access, MFA, secrets management and regular audits. Your code and data remain your property.

How quickly can we start?

After discovery we assign a pilot team within days to establish cadence.

How does pricing work for back-end tasks?

Covered under standard retainers. Allocate hours across front- and back-end as needed.

Code ownership

You own all code in your repositories. We collaborate via pull requests.
